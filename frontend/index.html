How It Works

Each donation is sent directly to the chosen foundation. For every ether donated you earn 10 ECO tokens as a thank you for supporting environmental causes.

Connect your wallet to track your ECO balance and view your full donation history.

About ECO Coin

ECO is an ERC20 token with a fixed supply issued by this platform. You can hold it as a proof of your contributions or transfer it just like any other token.
